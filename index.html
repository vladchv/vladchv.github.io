Fortnightly stay

If you book your mooring for 15 days or more, you’ll get a 5% discount on the daily rate.  Don’t miss out and stop off at l’Escala! Promotion valid from 1st May to 30th September.

Seasonal berthing

Book your mooring from 1st May to 31st October.

  • 5% discount on the Winter Pack with Varador l’Escala: 6 months of wintering + maintenance of the hull underbody
  • 10% discount if your boat is less than 7 metres long
  • 1 guided route by dinghy sailing and double kayak rental (2h/2 people) at the Club’s Sailing School
  • Payment in instalments: 50% at the beginning of the service and 50% on 1st July.

Winter berthing

  • Mooring booked from 1st October to 30th April
  • 10% discount on the monthly rental price during the low season
  • Payment of the service: 50% at the beginning of the booking 50% balance on 1st January.

Annual berthing

  • 5% discount for early payment before January 31st
  • 20% discount on the second access card at the port. If you are not yet a client of ours, check the conditions of this service with the Main Office
  • Voucher worth €100 exchangeable for sports services at the Club’s Sailing School
  • Special conditions for the services of Varador l’Escala such as the Express Maintenance Pack or the Varador l’Escala Hull Underbody Treatment. Check the benefits with Port Management or at the offices of Varador l’Escala
  • Get 1 free night in the ports that are members of the Catalan Association of Marinas and Tourism who are participating in the campaign “Navegar té premi” (Sailing is rewarding). Check the details with the Main Office
  • Get a 2nd night free per year at Port d’Agde et du Cap and Port Leucate.

about

Everything you need to go out and enjoy sailing activities as well as discover the heart of the Costa Brava at your fingertips!

Services for members and users:

  • Water and electricity at the mooring.
  • Access to the Club’s bathrooms and showers.
  • Docking service 24/7.
  • Broadband WIFI service.
  • Social Club and reading room/library.
  • Mooring assistance.
  • Internal transport service with electric vehicle.
  • Waiting dock.
  • VHF channel 9.
  • Infirmary / Cardio protected space
  • Laundry.
  • Selective waste collection
  • Gray, black and bilge water extraction service.
  • Petrol station with a waiting dock of 70m length.
  • Fuel reserve for large boats.
  • Sale of oil, ice and drinks.
  • Boat ramp service.
  • Crane and travelift service in Varador l’Escala.
  • Bar & Restaurant.
  • Commercial area with shops and nautical services.

Request from Dock Staff:

  • Boat towing.
  • Charging batteries for starting engines.
  • Underwater services.
  • Supply of cleats and mooring ropes, adjustment and revision of mooring systems.
  • Maintenance of facilities.
  • Rental and sale of electrical adapters.

Request from Club’s Yacht Office:

  • Paid access to the Club’s car park for vehicles and trailers.
  • Conference Room and Multipurpose Room.
  • Rental and purchase of cards and access keys to the Club’s facilities.
  • Collection and delivery of post and parcels.
  • Telephone.
  • Tourist information.
  • Meteorological information.
  • Bike rental.
  • Booking taxis and rental vehicles.

Follow the following recommendations to make good use of the port:

  • Regulate the speed and do not exceed 3 Kts.
  • Reduce water consumption.
  • Reduce electricity consumption.
  • Remove your waste correctly.
  • Use the suction pump located at the Gas Station to empty the waste water tanks of your boat.

Tips to enjoy the sea responsibly:

  • Responsible Navigation in Natural Parks
  • Responsible Fishing Regulations in Natural Parks
  • Protect the posidonia

Varador l’Escala

Located inside the port of l’Escala, Varador l’Escala is a company specialising in the services of hoisting, dry docking, cleaning, sandblasting, application of antifouling and winter storage of boats.  Its recently modernised premises have a travel lift for moving vessels up to 60 tonnes and 20m in length, as well as a lift truck and crane for boats that weigh less than six tonnes.

Services

  • Hull underbody cleaning.
  • Antifouling application.
  • Repair and painting of hull upperdeck.
  • Hull underbody repair and polishing with sand
  • Osmosis treatment.
  • Fibreglass repair.
  • General mechanics.
  • Boats winter storage.

what to see

Restaurant La Barra del Port

Come and try what our chef Eduardo Puig has to offer at the Club’s restaurant La Barra del Port. Healthy and balanced Mediterranean cuisine with a personal touch. Seasonal products and local food that will delight your palate while enjoying the best views of the sea of Empuries and of the port.

The restaurant has two outdoor terraces, a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, a great gastronomic selection to come and enjoy at any time of day. Breakfast, bar service, cocktails, lunchtime menus, à la carte service and tapas so you can have a good time with your family and friends… but also a perfect space for hosting conventions or your most special celebrations.

For more information and reservations, check the website of La Barra del Port.

The Sea shop

Spare part, an accessory, bait or something for fishing. You can also hire boat-cleaning services at the Sea Shop and rent motorboats and sailing boats through the Novaimport company. If you are a member, you’ll get a 10% discount on clothes and accessories in the Sea Shop.
